Main Projects

  • Modern Warfare Cubed

    Modern Warfare Cubed is a collaborative project that I am proud to be a part of. This is a fork of the renowned Vic's Modern Warfare, and as its official successor, it adds a plethora of new features, including an extensive array of modern weaponry, vehicles, and more.

    As the head developer, I work closely with a team of 12 talented individuals. Together, we share a commitment to optimize and enhance this mod for the Minecraft community. We've succeeded in making it faster and lighter than Vic's Modern Warfare, a testament to our dedication to performance.

    Our team places a high value on compatibility and user convenience. We've implemented numerous quality-of-life features to improve the overall gaming experience.

    Java Lang Markdown

  • Fancier Block Particles

    Fancier Block Particles is my personal take on enhancing Minecraft's aesthetics. I've replaced the standard 2D particles with more visually appealing 3D particles. A new, elegant block placing animation adds to the visual overhaul.

    It's a fork of the popular Fancy Block Particles, but I've ensured it offers significant improvements. My version of the mod runs 276% faster, loads 25% faster, and uses less RAM, VRAM, CPU, and GPU resources, enhancing performance and efficiency.

    The upcoming version of Fancier Block Particles is my most ambitious update yet. It's designed to be even faster than Minecraft's default particle renderer, pushing the boundaries of performance and visual appeal in the game!

    Java Lang Markdown

  • Opticraft

    Opticraft is a list of 1.12.2 Minecraft optimization mods that help users get the most of their experience without spending time fact checking claims.

    Before adding any mod to the list, they are meticulously vetted to ensure their safety and effectiveness.

    The process includes scrutinizing their source code (if available), as well as testing and benchmarking them to verify their performance and usefulness.

    HTML CSS Markdown

Projects

  • Re-Crystallized Wing

    Re-Crystallized Wing is a Minecraft mod made by me that adds 3 magical wings and a staff to the game.

    Re-Crystallized Wing is a 1.12.2 port of Crystal Wing by bspkrs from 1.8.9, which itself was a port of Crystal Wing by DaftPVF from 1.2.5.

    With in depth configs, you can customize everything, the defaults are for a more modern and balanced experience, there are also config options tailored for nostalgia like the nostalgic sounds from Crystal Wing 1.2.5.

    Java Lang

  • Mystic Staffs

    Mystic Staffs is a Minecraft mod made by me for a commision that adds 10 magical staffs to the game.

    Are you tired of over-produced and under-polished magical elements in popular 1.12.2 mods? Do you wish magical abilities didn't involve 10 GUI elements and countless crafting recipes?

    Mystical Staffs adds 10 vanilla-friendly Staffs, useful for modpack devs. These staffs have no crafting recipes but can be added through CraftTweaker or similar mods.

    Java Lang
